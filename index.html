Making Dubai More Livable Every Day

About Us

Dubai Municipality is committed to enhancing the quality of life for all its citizens, visitors, and residents. With a vision to create a sustainable and livable city, we work tirelessly to provide essential services, advance infrastructure projects, and maintain the city's green spaces.

Our mission is to lead the way in creating a world-class city by delivering innovative, effective, and customer-centric solutions that contribute to the well-being of the community.

Our Services

At Dubai Municipality, we offer a wide range of services designed to support and improve the everyday lives of residents, businesses, and visitors. From waste management to urban planning, our services ensure a clean, sustainable, and well-maintained city.

Waste Management

Comprehensive waste collection and recycling programs to keep the city clean and environmentally friendly.

Public Parks

Beautifully maintained parks and green spaces for families and individuals to enjoy outdoor activities.

Building Permits

Efficient processing and management of building permits and regulations to support urban development.

Easy Payment Solutions

We make paying for municipal services quick and convenient with our multiple payment options. Choose from our secure online platforms or visit one of our service centers across Dubai.

Online Payments

Pay your bills and fines easily through our secure online portal, available 24/7 for your convenience.

Service Centers

Visit our service centers across the city for in-person assistance with your payments and other inquiries.

Open Data

Dubai Municipality is committed to transparency and providing access to valuable data. Our Open Data initiative promotes research, innovation, and development by sharing public data resources.

City Statistics

Access detailed statistics about population, infrastructure, and public services.

Environmental Reports

Explore data on air quality, waste management, and sustainability efforts.

Urban Development Plans

Review future plans for urban development, including zoning and infrastructure projects.
